About Us

Welcome to the Singapore Salary & Resignation Calculator Portal.

Our Mission

Navigating labor laws can be daunting for both local employees and HR professionals in Singapore. With the major 2026 updates implemented by the Ministry of Manpower (MOM)—including the new $8,000 CPF Ordinary Wage ceiling and updated Local Qualifying Salary parameters—we realized there was a critical need for an automated, lightweight, and transparent verification tool.

Why Use Our Tool?

  • Up-to-Date: Fully compliant with May 2026 MOM regulatory criteria.
  • Privacy First: Built using vanilla client-side code. Your salary information never leaves your device.
  • HR Ready: Features professional-grade layout print systems for pay statement generation.

Open Source Integrity

This utility portal is operated with independent open-source coding guidelines. We aim to protect workforce rights and streamline monthly salary calculations across cross-border tech and retail communities.
