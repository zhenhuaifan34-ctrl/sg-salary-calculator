Singapore Salary Benchmark Guide 2026: Compare Pay the Right Way

Use this guide to benchmark your salary beyond a simple monthly number. It explains how to compare annual cash, CPF, AWS, bonus, working hours and job scope.

Quick answer

A Singapore salary benchmark should compare total annual compensation, not just monthly basic salary. For employees, the most useful benchmark includes monthly salary, AWS, variable bonus, fixed allowances, employee CPF, employer CPF, working hours, industry and role seniority.

What “salary benchmark” really means

Many workers search for “average salary Singapore” or “salary benchmark Singapore”, but a single average number can be misleading. A junior executive, senior engineer, sales manager and finance analyst can have very different packages even at the same monthly salary.

Instead of asking whether one number is “high” or “low”, use a structured benchmark:

How to benchmark your salary in 2026

1. Start from your annual cash package

Monthly salary alone undercounts employees with AWS, reliable bonus or fixed allowances. Convert your salary into annual cash before comparing with job ads or recruiter ranges.

2. Calculate CPF and take-home pay

CPF rules can make two salaries feel different in monthly cash flow. In 2026, the full contribution rate for Singapore Citizens and third-year PRs aged 55 and below remains 20% employee and 17% employer for monthly wages above S$750, subject to CPF wage ceilings.

3. Compare job scope, not title only

A “manager” title can mean people management in one company and individual contributor work in another. Benchmark scope: revenue responsibility, team size, regional exposure, complexity and required skills.

4. Adjust for hours and commute

A higher salary can be weaker if it creates a large time burden. For employees comparing offers, use the offer comparison tool to calculate effective hourly value.

Salary benchmark checklist

  • What is my monthly basic salary?
  • Do I receive AWS or only discretionary bonus?
  • What is my annual cash package?
  • What is my net monthly cash after employee CPF?
  • How many hours do I really work per week?
  • How much commute time and cost does this job require?
  • Is my role scope similar to the benchmark I am using?

FAQ

Should I benchmark salary before or after CPF?

Benchmark market salary before CPF, then calculate take-home pay separately. Both numbers matter for different reasons.

Is annual compensation better than monthly salary?

Yes. Annual compensation captures AWS, bonus and allowances, so it is better for job offer comparison.

Can I use job ads as salary benchmarks?

Yes, but treat them as ranges. Confirm whether the posted range includes AWS, bonus, allowances or only basic salary.
