Our calculator operates entirely within your web browser (client-side). Any financial information, salaries, age groups, or overtime hours you enter are processed instantly by JavaScript on your device. We do not transmit, store, or save your financial data to any external server.

Our tool utilizes browser localStorage strictly to store your calculation history logs for your own convenience. This data remains entirely on your personal device and can be cleared at any time by clicking the "Clear All" button on the interface.
