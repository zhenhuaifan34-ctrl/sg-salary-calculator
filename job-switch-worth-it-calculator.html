Frequently asked questions

Is a 10% salary increase enough to switch jobs?

Not always. Compare AWS, bonus, CPF, annual leave, commute time, working hours, job stability and switching cost.

Should I include employer CPF as cash?

No. Employer CPF is valuable, but it is not immediate cash paid into your bank account.

How is break-even time calculated?

Break-even time is estimated as switching cost divided by monthly value gain. If the new offer has no monthly gain, break-even may be shown as not reached.

Does this calculator include income tax?

No. Singapore income tax depends on tax residency, reliefs and other income.

Can I use this for EP or S Pass offers?

Yes, choose “No CPF calculation” if CPF is not applicable.
