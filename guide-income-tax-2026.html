Singapore Income Tax Guide 2026: Tax Rates, Filing & Salary Planning

Singapore has one of the world's most competitive personal income tax systems. Understanding how income tax works can help employees estimate their actual take-home income more accurately.

💰 Tax Fact:
Singapore uses a progressive income tax system. Higher income levels are taxed at higher marginal rates, while lower income levels enjoy lower tax rates.

Who Needs to Pay Income Tax?

Singapore tax residents are generally required to pay tax on income earned in Singapore. Tax obligations vary depending on residency status and income level.

How Taxable Income Is Calculated

Taxable Income = Employment Income − Tax Reliefs − Allowable Deductions

Income Tax Filing Period

Most individuals submit their income tax returns between March and April each year through IRAS.

How CPF Affects Income Tax

CPF contributions may reduce taxable income in certain circumstances and are an important part of overall financial planning.

  • Understand taxable employee benefits
  • Review available tax reliefs
